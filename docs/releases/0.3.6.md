# Short-Stay Accounts 0.3.6

- Investment Recovery view under Financial Position.
- Asset funding classification for owner, loan, business-income, and mixed funding.

Existing local data, passwords, bookings, and reports remain in place. Existing assets are marked Not classified until their funding source is selected.
